Star Wars Trilogy

There are six movies in the original trilogy of Star Wars.

Sequel Trilogy

There are three movies that followed the original trilogy of Star Wars.


Star Wars IV: A New Hope

Directed by George Lucas

Released on August 23, 1977

Mark Hamill
Harrison Ford
Carrie Fisher

Star Wars is a 1977 American epic space-opera film written and directed by George Lucas, produced by Lucasfilm and distributed by 20th Century Fox. It is the first film in the Star Wars film series and fourth chronological chapter of the "Skywalker Saga".

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars V: The Empire Strikes Back

Directed by Irvin Kershner

Released on May 21, 1980

Mark Hamill
Harrison Ford
Carrie Fisher

The Empire Strikes Back is a 1980 American epic space-opera film directed by Irvin Kershner from a screenplay by Leigh Brackett and Lawrence Kasdan, based on a story by George Lucas. The sequel to Star Wars, it is the second film in the Star Wars film series and the fifth chronological chapter of the "Skywalker Saga".

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars VI: Return of The Jedi

Directed by Richard Marquand

Released on May 25, 1983

Mark Hamill
Harrison Ford
Carrie Fisher

Luke Skywalker (Mark Hamill) battles horrible Jabba the Hut and cruel Darth Vader to save his comrades in the Rebel Alliance and triumph over the Galactic Empire. Han Solo (Harrison Ford) and Princess Leia (Carrie Fisher) reaffirm their love and team with Chewbacca, Lando Calrissian (Billy Dee Williams), the Ewoks and the androids C-3PO and R2-D2 to aid in the disruption of the Dark Side and the defeat of the evil emperor.

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars I: A Phantom Menace

Directed by George Lucas

Released on June 30, 1999

Ewan McGregor
Liam Neeson
Jake Lloyd
Samuel L. Jackson

Star Wars: Episode I – The Phantom Menace is a 1999 American epic space opera film written and directed by George Lucas. It starred Liam Neeson, Ewan McGregor, Natalie Portman, Jake Lloyd, Ahmed Best, Ian McDiarmid, Anthony Daniels, Kenny Baker, Pernilla August, and Frank Oz.

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars II: Attack of The Clones

Directed by George Lucas

Released on May 16, 2002

Hayden Christensen
Ewan McGregor
Natalie Portman

Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clones is a 2002 American epic space opera film directed by George Lucas and written by Lucas and Jonathan Hales. The sequel to The Phantom Menace, it is the fifth film in the Star Wars film series and second chronological chapter of the "Skywalker Saga".

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars III: Revenge of The Sith

Directed by George Lucas

Released on May 19, 2005

Hayden Christensen
Ewan McGregor
Natalie Portman

Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith is a 2005 American epic space opera film written and directed by George Lucas. It stars Ewan McGregor, Natalie Portman, Hayden Christensen, Ian McDiarmid, Samuel L. Jackson, Christopher Lee, Anthony Daniels, Kenny Baker and Frank Oz.

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars VII: The Force Awakens

Directed by J.J. Abrams

Released on December 17, 2015

Daisy Ridley
John Boyega
Adam Driver
Harrison Ford
Carrie Fisher

Thirty years after the defeat of the Galactic Empire, Han Solo (Harrison Ford) and his young allies face a new threat from the evil Kylo Ren (Adam Driver) and the First Order.

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars VIII: The Last Jedi

Directed by Rian Johnson

Released on December 15, 2017

Daisy Ridley
John Boyega
Adam Driver
Mark Hamill
Carrie Fisher

Star Wars: The Last Jedi is a 2017 American epic space opera film written and directed by Rian Johnson.

Copyright of Lucasfilm

Star Wars IX: The Rise of Skywalker

Directed by J.J. Abrams

Released on December 20, 2019

Daisy Ridley
John Boyega
Adam Driver
Mark Hamill
Carrie Fisher

Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker is a 2019 American epic space opera film produced, co-written, and directed by J. J. Abrams.
